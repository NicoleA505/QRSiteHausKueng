Farm to Table

We try to offer as many regional products as possible on the buffet, because we love and appreciate our home.

Honey

We pride ourselves on offering local natural honeys made in Langen am Arlberg from Walter Stroltz who is a beekeeper and ski guide.

Chives

We harvest our chives every summer in the Pazueltal, to add a special mountain taste to our scrambled eggs.

Marmelade

Our delicious homemade jams are made from the wild blueberries that we pick in summer, sometimes finding more than we bargained for!

Bacon

Our bacon comes from our own pigs who roam the mountains in summer.

Fresh Bread

Our fresh bread is delivered daily to our door from the bakery in Lech

Milk & Cheese

The specialty cheeses and meats from our continental buffet are delivered locally from Ländle Milch, which is a dairy cooperative belonging to all the local farmers of Vorarlberg. 'Bergkäse', which literally translates to "Mountain Cheese" is made from the milk of local grazing cows.
